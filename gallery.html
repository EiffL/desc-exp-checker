Gallery

Every once in a while, there's a nugget of gold in a lot of dust. While we are primarily interested in finding image artifacts, it would be stupid to let out on the opportunity of spotting a nice star cluster, an asteroid, or a group of interacting galaxies ...
Here we highlight these awesome objects, all found by our users. To report them, use the selector in the viewer, describe what you see, and click on the object in the image. That's all you have to do to get entry to the Hall of Fame.
